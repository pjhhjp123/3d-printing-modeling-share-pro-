3D Printing Materials Information
  • PLA (Polylactic Acid)
  • Biodegradable plastic
  • Low-temperature printing (180-220°C)
  • Easy to use and non-toxic
  • ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene)
  • High durability and strength
  • Strong odor possible
  • High-temperature printing required (230-260°C)
  • PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol)
  • High durability and transparency
  • Food safety compliant
  • Medium temperature printing required (220-250°C)
  • TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane)
  • Very flexible and excellent elasticity
  • Good shock absorption
  • Slower print speed possible (210-230°C)
